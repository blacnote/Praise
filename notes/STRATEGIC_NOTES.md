# ChurchSense — Strategic Notes & Differentiation

*Companion to the Phase 1 Blueprint. Honest assessment of where ChurchSense wins and where the risk lives.*

---

## The one-line thesis
ChurchSense is not "another playback app." Its entire reason to exist is **Spirit Flow** — letting worship breathe (hold, loop, pray, vamp, extend) while click, guide, stems, and lyrics stay perfectly locked. Everything else is table stakes.

## Why the idea is strong
- **Real pain, unsolved.** Every existing rig (Ableton-based playback, Loop Community Playback, MultiTracks Prime, Prime/Playback apps) treats a service as a fixed timeline. When the leader is led to linger in the bridge and pray, the track marches to the outro anyway. Teams hack around it with awkward loop sections and a stressed tracks operator. ChurchSense names that pain and builds the whole product around it.
- **Right scoping.** No custom in-ear hardware in Phase 1. No lead-vocal stem by default (leader sings live). Stereo-bounce-to-StudioLive fallback so a real church can plug in on Sunday without a perfect setup.
- **A genuine thesis, not a slogan:** *ChurchSense follows worship. Worship does not follow ChurchSense.*

## Where the risk lives (be honest)
1. **Not first to the category.** Can't win on "stems + click + guide." Can *only* win on Spirit Flow. The MVP should feel almost minimal on playback and excellent on flow. Lead with what nobody else does.
2. **Seamless looping/holding is the hard bet.** Looping click+guide+stems+lyrics in real time with zero glitch, while the leader prays, is the entire technical wager. If it feels janky even once on a Sunday, trust is gone. **Prototype this first**, before the set builder polish.
3. **Stem sourcing is a quiet dependency.** Churches buy stems from MultiTracks/Loop Community — who are also competitors. Decide early where stems come from and in what format.

## Build-order recommendation
1. Prove Spirit Flow audio core (hold/loop, no glitch) — the riskiest, most differentiating piece.
2. Wrap it in the Set Builder + Live view.
3. Add routing outputs (house clean; click/guide isolated; stereo bounce).
4. Layer ProPresenter sync (loose, resilient, degrades gracefully).

## Guard rail
The danger is drift into "a nicer playback app." Protect Spirit Flow as the heart of every release.
