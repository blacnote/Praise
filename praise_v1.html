DLGSENSE Product

ChurchSense

A worship-service operating system for the local church.
ChurchSense is the brain of the worship service — it plans the set, plays separated stems, generates click and guide for the team's ears, keeps the house mix clean, and stays in step with ProPresenter — while always leaving the worship leader free to follow where the Spirit leads.
ChurchSense follows worship. Worship does not follow ChurchSense.

Phase 1 · What's in

  • Set Builder — songs, order, total/elapsed/remaining, jump to any song
  • Song data — title, key, BPM, lyrics, sections + lengths
  • Click + Guide — count-ins, spoken cues — team ears only
  • Stem playback — drums, bass, keys, pads, etc. (no lead vocal)
  • House mix — stays clean of click + guide, by design
  • Routing concept — house, click, guide, stereo bounce to console
  • ProPresenter sync — concept-level, loose & resilient

Phase 1 · What's out

  • Custom in-ear / personal monitor hardware
  • Per-member monitor mixing
  • Building our own stem-separation engine
  • Lighting, video, or recording control
  • Mobile remote-control for the whole team
  • Cloud song marketplace / multi-site sync
  • Automatic transcription / AI lyric generation

✦ Why Spirit Flow Matters

  • Most music software treats a service like a backing track: press play, the song runs start to finish on a timeline.
  • Real worship does not work that way. The Spirit may lead the worship leader to hold a moment, pray, vamp on a bridge, or extend a song past the plan.
  • Spirit Flow Mode lets the leader linger without fighting the software — and the band, the lyrics, and the clock all stay in sync.

v1 · Honest Status

  • This is a self-contained HTML proof-of-concept. No build step, no install.
  • Stems are synthesized tones — not real separated audio files yet.
  • True multi-output routing needs a desktop layer (browsers can't address multiple interfaces).
  • ProPresenter sync is a status placeholder, not a live link.
  • Set Builder, Live/Run, and Spirit Flow (Hold / Loop / Prayer / Resume) do work as a POC.
